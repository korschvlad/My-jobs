Les Feixes de Coaner

Tranquillity·Nature - History

A sixteenth-century fortified country house, a place to enjoy the tranquillity, nature and history that is present in every corner.

The house consists of two separate parts, one in which the owners live and where the other tenants formerly lived, now restored with great love to be able to offer as accommodation.

The house has accommodation for 11 people, 4 bedrooms with bathroom (three triples and one double). Fully equipped kitchen (refrigerator, microwave, oven, dishwasher, washing machine and small appliances). Cot and high chairs for babies.
